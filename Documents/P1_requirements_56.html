Administrative details

  • Client name: Bestemor Borghild
  • Contact person: Fictional
  • Type of company: Fictional personal pastry blog
  • Page name: Bestemor Borghild's Bakebonanza

Purpose and goals

The purpose is to showcase and share Borghild's prize-winning family recipes to the world. We want to inspire a sense of love and joy for baking by using tried and tested recipes, with the highly esteemed stamp of approval from Borghild herself.

Borghild wants the site to be simple, intuitive, and as efficient as possible, so that everyone will give her recipes a chance.

In addition, Borghild will accept questions regarding her recipes, as well as user-submitted recipes, so she can review them and perhaps even add them to her list of user-approved recipes.

Audience

Since baking is a dying art, Bestemor Borghild wants to incite some excitement from the young generation who has recently moved out from their childhood homes and wants to carry the baking torch further into the future. The target group is thus people aged 18-28 with an interest and little to some prior knowledge about baking; specifically those who want a taste of some good old-fashioned "grandma's recipes" without requiring one.

Because of the target age group, the page will have to be designed to work just as well on a mobile device as on a desktop computer.

Content of the Website and its Organization

The page will serve as a digital recipe book, where visitors get an overview of the recipes Borghild posts. One can then click each individual recipe to see detailed information about the recipe.

Front page
The front page will serve as a gateway to all the other pages, together with showcasing some recipes in a slideshow and introducing the visitor's to Borghild in an "about" section as well. This allows the visitor to feel more familiar to Borghild, turning her into everyone's favorite baking grandma. The design of the front page will therefore be straightforward: serving as a gateway, and introducing the visitor to Borghild.
Recipe list
The recipe list displays a list of all of the recipes that Borghild posts. This page only has key information about each recipe displayed as a card, and by clicking one of them, the visitor will be taken to the specific recipe page.
Specific recipe
Page detailing the recipe, both the ingredients and an easy to understand step-by-step guide to craft the tasty pastry. The page also includes illustrations and external resources for any necessary information.
User-submitted recipes
A page similar to the recipe list, but solely with recipes from users with the very valuable stamp of approval from Borghild.
Baking 101
A dedicated page to the basics of baking and baking techniques, presented in a suitable way. This could be a link to an external video or informational images/animations if a detailed text description is insufficient.
Contact
Form to submit either questions or recipes directed towards Borghild. A simple page with just the necessary elements.

Requirements

Functional

  • The page has to be able to receive recipe suggestions and questions from any user of the page.
  • A selection of recipe images has to be displayed in a slideshow on the front page.
  • The content must be accessible through links on the page.
  • The header must be available from all pages and allow the user to return to any main category (front page, recipe list, user recipes, Baking 101, or contact).

Non-functional

  • The site should load within 3 seconds on a 10 mb/s connection
  • The website should be easy to maintain
  • Every page should be accessible in 4 clicks or less
  • Responsive design should make the site pleasent to use on both mobile and desktop.
  • The website has to be usable in all major browsers and be usable on both mobile and desktop.
